Our Story

A School Built on Two Passions

Harmony and Health Microschool was born from a simple but powerful belief: children thrive when they are seen as individuals, not numbers in a classroom. Founded by a certified music teacher and a professional fitness, health & wellness coach, our school brings together personalized academics, artistic expression, and physical well-being in one nurturing community.

Harmony

Music is a language every child can speak. Our curriculum integrates music education into daily learning, nurturing creativity, discipline, and self-expression from the very beginning.

Health

A healthy body supports a healthy mind. Physical education, nutrition awareness, and wellness habits are woven throughout every school day, not treated as extras.

Learning

Every child's academic journey is uniquely their own. Our personalized, technology-assisted curriculum adapts to each learner's pace, strengths, and individual goals.

Questions & Answers

Frequently Asked Questions

We know choosing the right school for your child is one of the most important decisions you'll make. Here are answers to the questions we hear most from families.

A microschool is a small, intentional learning community (think of it as the best of both worlds between homeschooling and a traditional school). Students receive the personalized attention and flexible pacing of homeschooling, within a structured, social, and educator-led environment.

While students are registered as homeschoolers with the state of Florida, they attend our learning center daily with qualified educators, follow a structured daily schedule, and learn alongside peers. It combines the legal flexibility of homeschooling with the community and accountability of a school.

Each student is assessed individually and assigned their own digital curriculum that adapts to their level, learning style, and pace. No two students follow the exact same path. A child can advance quickly in math while taking more time in reading, without ever being held back or left behind.

Students cycle through personalized academic work, music instruction with Mrs. Lambe-Estevez, and physical education and wellness sessions with Mr. Estevez, all within a structured daily schedule designed to balance focused learning with movement and creativity.

We are intentionally small by design. In our first year we will accept a maximum of 12 students. This ensures every child receives genuine individual attention from our educators every single day.

We serve children in Kindergarten through 5th Grade, ages 5 to 11. Each child is assessed upon enrollment so their curriculum begins exactly where they are, regardless of their grade level.

Students are registered as home-educated learners with the state of Florida, which is fully legal and recognized. Florida has a well-established homeschool framework, and families maintain full compliance with state education requirements through our program.
